# Jisui - kindle2pdf 📚✨

A dedicated tool to convert Kindle books into high-quality PDFs, optimized for **reMarkable 2** and other E-Ink tablets.

## 📖 The Story Behind the Project

<img src="https://github.com/RyuheiRay/Jisui-kindle2pdf/blob/master/src/assets/Jisui-kindle2pdf.JPG?raw=true" align="right" width="400" hspace="20">

As a reMarkable 2 lover, my biggest frustration was the inability to read my Kindle library on that beautiful E-Ink screen. I developed this tool to bridge that gap. 

Initially, I kept this project private for personal use. However, I realized that in the age of AI, having your library in a versatile PDF format is more valuable than ever. I decided to go public to help fellow E-Ink enthusiasts enjoy their books without limits.

## 🛠 Requirements
* **OS:** Windows only.
* **Software:** [Kindle for PC](https://www.amazon.com/kindle-apps/b?node=16571048011) must be installed.

---

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Download:** Get the latest version from the [Releases](https://github.com/) page.
2. **Launch:** Extract the ZIP and run `Jisui-kindle2pdf.exe`.
   * *Note: On the first launch, Windows Defender SmartScreen may show a warning. Click **"More info"** and then **"Run anyway"**.*
3. **Open Kindle:** Click the **"Open Kindle"** button in the app to launch Kindle for PC.
4. **Prepare the Book:** Open the book you wish to convert.
5. **Adjust Layout:** Go to **View > Show Display Options (Ctrl + Shift + D)** in Kindle. Adjust the **"Page Width"** so the text fits nicely for your device. 
   * *Tip: Once set, this usually works for all other books. Supporters get a visual guide to make this even easier!*
6. **Convert:** Click **"START CONVERSION"**. 
   * **⚠️ IMPORTANT:** Do not touch your PC during this process. The app automatically captures pages one by one.
7. **Done:** Click **"View Results"** to check your new PDF!

### 🌟 Exclusive Supporter Features
* **Output Quality:** Adjust PDF compression. Lower numbers create smaller files—perfect for saving space on your tablet.
* **High Contrast:** Enhances text visibility specifically for E-Ink screens.
* **Grayscale:** Converts images to grayscale to slightly reduce file size.
* **Always on Top:** Keeps the tool window visible even when Kindle is in full-screen.
* **Auto-open Kindle:** Automatically launches Kindle for PC when you start the tool.
* **Kindle Setup Tool:** Provides a visual guide (green frame) showing the exact capture area for your Target Device (e.g., reMarkable 2), making "Page Width" adjustment effortless.
